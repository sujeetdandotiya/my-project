WHY GYANDEEP MIDDLE SCHOOL

Our vision is to make Gyandeep Middle School a learning community where motivated students and staff come together to participate in active learning facilitated by the use of modern technology. We aim to develop young individuals who think, question, investigate and learn through enquiry based learning and project work. We will ensure the fostering of self-discipline, mental flexibility, positive attitude, independent thinking, thoughtful decision-making, critical analysis & appreciation and understanding of differences of opinion among other things.
